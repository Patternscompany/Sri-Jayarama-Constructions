WE ARE CONSTRUCTION COMPANY

Building Trust, Quality & Value for Generations

SriRama–Jayarama Group is a well-known establishment in the erstwhile Mahabubnagar district, with a strong legacy in multiple automobile segments including Mahindra Tractors, Maruti Suzuki Cars, TVS Two Wheelers, Total ELF Lubricants, Mahindra Construction Equipment, Shaktiman Agri Implements, SriRama Trailers and Agri Implements. Established in 1974, the group has achieved remarkable milestones across all the industries it has stepped into, driven by its “Customer First” philosophy and decades of trust built with the people of Mahbubnagar.

With a successful journey of 40+ years, the group expanded into the real estate sector through Sri Jayarama Constructions Pvt. Ltd. in 2016. The vision is clear — to offer premium, luxury housing to the people of Mahbubnagar at fair and justified prices. Under this mission, SriRama Landmark was launched with the goal of building Duplexes, Independent Houses, and Apartments enriched with modern amenities that match the standards of Hyderabad.

With deep roots in our automobile industry values — quality, trust, and customer satisfaction — Sri Jayarama Constructions has achieved tremendous support from the community. Without any major public marketing campaigns, our duplexes and independent homes were sold purely through customer trust and word-of-mouth, demonstrating the strong belief people have in the SriRama–Jayarama brand.

With Phase 2 underway on an additional 50 acres adjacent to Phase 1, we are committed to elevating our standards even further and creating a new benchmark in the real estate industry of Mahbubnagar. Our focus remains the same — delivering high-quality homes with honesty, transparency, and long-lasting value.

Over the years, Sri Jayarama Constructions has earned a reputation for delivering projects with uncompromised quality. With upcoming expansions, innovative layouts, improved amenities, and community-driven planning, Sri Jayarama Constructions is committed to becoming a leader in shaping modern, high-quality living spaces for future generations.
